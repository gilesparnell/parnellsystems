# Business Overview

*Last updated: March 2026*

---

## AllConvos (allconvos.ai)

**What it is:** AI voice receptionist and SMS automation for small-medium businesses. When a customer calls a business, the AI answers, qualifies the lead, books appointments, and sends follow-up SMS — all without human involvement.

**Current state:**
- 4 active paying clients (gyms primarily)
- Net negative revenue, self-funded, finite runway
- Partnership with Jesse dissolved March 2026 — Gilo now operating independently
- Onboarding currently takes ~2 weeks (target: 10-15 minutes, zero-touch)

**Tech stack:**
- GoHighLevel (GHL) — CRM, workflows, SMS
- Assistable AI — voice agents (account access issue with Wateeb, March 2026)
- VAPI — preferred voice platform going forward
- Thinker AI — voice agents
- Twilio — phone numbers and SMS
- Vercel — deployment
- Stripe — payments

**Revenue model:** Monthly recurring subscription. Currently using stripe, aiming for upfront payment model.

**Target markets (priority order):**
1. Gyms / fitness studios (current clients, proven value)
2. Tradies / roofers / HVAC / solar (high ticket = high missed-call cost)
3. Medical clinics
4. Any SMB where missed calls = lost revenue

**Key product goal:** Zero-touch provisioning
- Customer fills web form → automated knowledge base creation → live agent in 10-15 min
- No human involvement in onboarding process

## Parnell.Systems (parnellsystems.com)

**What it is:** Systems consulting for SMEs. Audit, architect, implement operational infrastructure. New offering: Intelligence Layer (AI OS for operators).

**Current state:**
- Website live (React/Vite, Vercel)
- Intelligence Layer product page built and live
- No active consulting clients yet — building pipeline

**Services:**
1. System Design & Automation
2. AI for Customer Handling
3. Workflow & Tool Optimisation
4. Visibility & Reporting
5. Intelligence Layer (new — AI OS setup + monthly retainer)

**Go-to-market:** Free system audit → engagement → monthly retainer

## The Relationship Between the Two Businesses

- AllConvos is the product (external-facing, AI for customers)
- Parnell.Systems is the consulting layer (internal-facing, AI for operations)
- Intelligence Layer is the bridge — it uses the same tech as AllConvos but faces inward
- Both businesses validate each other's credibility
